Facilities provided by Planning Platinum for In-Person Events:


Venue Selection: Planning Platinum offers a curated selection of venues tailored to the specific needs and preferences of our clients. Whether you're looking for a sophisticated ballroom, a picturesque outdoor setting, or a unique and unconventional space, we have options to suit every style and budget. Our experienced team will work closely with you to understand your vision and objectives, and we will guide you through the venue selection process to find the perfect location for your event.


Logistics Management: From transportation to accommodations, Planning Platinum handles all logistical arrangements to ensure that your event runs smoothly from start to finish. We coordinate with vendors and service providers to arrange transportation for your guests, book accommodations for out-of-town attendees, and manage any other logistical details that may arise. With our expertise and attention to detail, you can trust us to take care of all the logistics so you can focus on enjoying your event.


On-Site Coordination: On the day of your event, Planning Platinum provides on-site coordination to ensure that everything goes according to plan. Our experienced team will be on hand to oversee the setup and execution of your event, coordinating with vendors, managing timelines, and addressing any issues that may arise. With our on-site coordination services, you can relax and enjoy your event knowing that everything is being taken care of by our dedicated team.


Catering Services: At Planning Platinum, we understand that delicious food and beverage options are essential to the success of any event. That's why we offer a wide range of catering services to suit every taste and dietary preference. Whether you're looking for gourmet cuisine, casual buffet options, or themed menus tailored to your event's theme, our experienced catering partners will work with you to create a menu that delights your guests and enhances the overall experience of your event.


Technical Support: For events requiring audiovisual equipment, lighting, and other technical support, Planning Platinum provides comprehensive technical services to ensure that everything runs smoothly. Our experienced technicians will set up and operate the necessary equipment, troubleshoot any issues that may arise, and ensure that your event goes off without a hitch. With our technical support services, you can rest assured that your event will be a success, no matter the technical requirements.
